## 1. The Problem: A Sinking Solution to a Rising Threat  
The world's oceans are our greatest ally in the fight against climate change. Coastal ecosystems like **mangroves, tidal marshes, and seagrass meadows** are carbon sequestration powerhouses‚Äîcapturing atmospheric carbon up to **10x faster than mature tropical forests**. This "Blue Carbon" remains locked in soil and sediment for millennia.  

‚ö†Ô∏è However, we are losing these critical habitats at alarming rates:  
- **340,000 to 980,000 hectares destroyed annually**  
- Up to **67% of historical mangrove coverage lost** in some regions  
- Once degraded, ecosystems shift from **carbon sinks to carbon sources**, releasing centuries of stored CO‚ÇÇ back into the atmosphere  

The **core issue** is a **visibility gap**. The immense value of blue carbon is abstract and difficult for policymakers, corporations, and the public to grasp. We need an **accessible tool** that quantifies this impact and visualizes the benefits of conservation and restoration.

## 2. Our Solution: oCarbn, an Interactive Insight Engine  
**oCarbn** is a **web-based platform** that makes the invisible, visible. It translates complex climate data into a clear, interactive, and actionable experience.  

‚úÖ Users can:  
- Explore global blue carbon ecosystems  
- Calculate their sequestration potential  
- Simulate real-world consequences of destruction vs. restoration  

**Mission:** To empower decision-makers at all levels‚Äî**from local communities to international corporations**‚Äîwith data-driven insights that drive protection and investment in our planet's most effective, yet most threatened, carbon sinks.

## 4. How It Works: User Journey  

1. **Explore** ‚Äì Navigate the globe to find a coastal region (e.g., **Sundarbans**).  
2. **Analyze** ‚Äì Click a mangrove forest ‚Üí dashboard shows area, carbon stock, global significance.  
3. **Simulate** ‚Äì Enter "1000 hectares" restoration project ‚Üí choose "Restore" scenario.  
4. **Visualize** ‚Äì Platform displays:  
   - Additional **~5,000 tonnes of CO‚ÇÇ** sequestered annually  
   - **20-year projection** of carbon locked away  
   - **Market value** of sequestration in carbon credits  
5. **Act** ‚Äì Get connected to **organizations** restoring the Sundarbans ‚Üí pathway from **insight to action**
